Grade Ranges

Course grades are assigned using the following ranges:

  • A = 95 or above
  • A- = 90 to 94.9
  • B+ = 87 to 89.9
  • B = 84 to 86.9

Minimum requirements are only adjusted downwards, for example, the threshold for a B+ may be lowered to 86 but it will not be raised to 88.
